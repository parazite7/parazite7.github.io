Thriving for decades

Northern Football Club has been active for over 36 years, with a loyal and welcoming community.

  • History

    Northern has won 9 senior premierships in 4 different divisions during the course of its history, the first in 1991 and the most recent in 2014.

  • Membership

    By becoming a Club Member today, you can show your support for Northern Football Club.

  • A thanks

    The Northern Football Club wishes to express its gratitude to the coaches for their unwavering support during the season and during the difficult covid period.

  • What we stand for

    Respect and Inclusiveness, Family and Community, Integrity and Openness are the core values of Northern Football Club.

Local to Northern Suburbs

The Northern Football Club is headquartered at Kings Park in Meadow Heights, Melbourne, and competes in the North District Football League (NDFL). We have senior, reserve, and under-19 teams, as well as numerous younger teams for both boys and girls, spanning from under 9 to under 17 years old.
